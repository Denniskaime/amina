History OF Gracious Foundation(GF)

Gracious Foundation has been in existence from the year 2013 to date. It was founded by Miss Grace Kagendo as a platform to give back to the society. The main diving motive was and has remained to avail equal opportunities for community members regardless of their backgrounds.
